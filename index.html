About Me

I’m 19 years old and currently living near MCU South Caloocan, where I’m part of the pioneering batch of BS Entertainment and Multimedia Computing, majoring in Digital Animation. Art has always been my way of expressing who I am. I’m a dancer, painter, and performer, and I also have a deep love for fashion and design. Whether it’s moving to the rhythm of music, bringing colors to life on a canvas, or creating something new through digital art — creativity is what drives me every day. You could say I’m an artist, but I like to think of myself as the “it girl” — confident, passionate, and unafraid to stand out. Welcome to my world of art, style, and self-expression!
